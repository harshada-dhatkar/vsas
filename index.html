Who Are We?

Established on December 17, 2014, Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha (VSAS) is a dynamic, youth-led nonprofit committed to empowering underprivileged communities in Pune. Leveraging a strong network of volunteers, VSAS addresses key areas like education, healthcare, skill development, career guidance, and sustainable livelihoods, creating meaningful change in the lives of marginalized individuals, particularly in rural regions.

Under the visionary leadership of Dr. Vishal Yuvraj Gurav, VSAS has consistently evolved to tackle the unique challenges faced by these communities. His unwavering dedication has positioned VSAS as a trusted NGO in Pune, driving social empowerment and fostering a more inclusive and equitable society.
