Who Are We?

Founded on December 17, 2014, Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha (VSAS) is a youth-driven nonprofit focused on empowering underprivileged communities in Pune. Through its volunteer network, VSAS works on education, health, skill development, career guidance, and sustainable livelihoods, transforming the lives of marginalized individuals, especially in rural areas.

Led by Dr. Vishal Yuvraj Gurav, VSAS has continuously adapted to meet the evolving challenges of underprivileged communities. His vision and dedication have made VSAS a respected NGO in Pune, focused on social upliftment and building a more inclusive society.
