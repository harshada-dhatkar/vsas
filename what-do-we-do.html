Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives!

Yogsutra Institute of Yoga and Research Center

Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha established the ‘Yogsutra Institute of Yoga & Research Center’ (Indian Yoga Association Associate Center No: IYA/AC/2020/053) to promote yoga education and certification for children. It offers opportunities for all age groups to embrace yoga as a lifestyle or profession, including free awareness programs like ‘Yoga for Immunity in COVID-19.

Donate Don’t Dump

Through the ‘Donate Don’t Dump’ program, VSAS collects used toys, stationery, and clothes from donors in and around Pune. These items are distributed to children and their families, offering support while promoting environmental conservation, reducing waste, and encouraging sustainable living.

VSAS Chiropractic Clinic

Provides chiropractic care services to enhance spinal health and overall well-being, ensuring access to quality healthcare for all.

VSAS Yoga and Fitness Studio

Delivers structured yoga and fitness classes for individuals of all ages and abilities, focusing on physical fitness and holistic well-being through personalized instruction.

Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao

Advocating for the education and empowerment of girls through initiatives focused on promoting gender equality and providing educational opportunities. Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" focuses on saving and educating the girl child, which is crucial for empowering women in society. Providing educational and financial support can make a significant difference in their futures.

Save Food (Anna Bachao)

Addressing food insecurity and hunger through initiatives aimed at food rescue, distribution, and promoting nutrition education.
