About Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha (VSAS)

Our History

Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha (VSAS) is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering the underprivileged sections of society. Established on December 17, 2014, VSAS has been working tirelessly across Pune, leveraging a strong network of passionate volunteers. The organization focuses on creating meaningful interventions in education, health, skills development, career guidance, and sustainable livelihoods, bringing transformative changes to the lives of children and youth.

Operating in rural areas where survival is a daily challenge, VSAS addresses critical issues faced by marginalized communities. Despite limited resources and complex, ever-evolving challenges, the team—under the dynamic leadership of Founder and Director Dr. Vishal Yuvraj Gurav—continues to demonstrate unwavering commitment to social upliftment. Dr. Gurav, an enthusiastic and motivated leader, ensures that VSAS remains adaptable and impactful, making it one of the most respected NGOs in Pune. Through innovative projects and a steadfast dedication to societal care, VSAS has established itself as a beacon of hope and change, driving progress for a more equitable and inclusive society.

About Founder

Dr. Vishal Yuvraj Gurav, founder of Vishal Sampurna Arogya Sanstha (VSAS), inspires underprivileged children through free yoga services. Qualified as an M.D. in Yoga & Naturopathy, Master in Psychology, Physiotherapist, Yoga Therapist, and Aerobics Dance Trainer, he aims to achieve "Sampurna Arogya" (complete health) for communities.

Thousands of slum children and women have benefited from his yoga and physiotherapy sessions. Starting social service at 16, Dr. Gurav continually enhances his expertise in psychology, sociology, and digital strategies to spread positivity.

His contributions have earned accolades, including:
  • Vishesh Sanman Chinh (Maharashtra Ratna Rajyastariy Sohla, 2022).
  • Honorary Doctorate in Yoga and Naturopathy (Human Rights Protection Commission, 2022).
  • Aayush Corona Yodha Award (MSME, 2021).
  • Karya Gaurav Puruskar (Police Friends Welfare Association, 2021).
  • Morya Gaurav Puraskar (2019).
  • Yoga Bhushan Puraskar (Morya Academy, 2021).
  • Guru Gaurav Puruskar (Jai Bhavani Pratishthan, 2021).

Through workshops and yoga demonstrations, Dr. Gurav inspires students and promotes his vision of health and well-being, creating lasting change.

Our timeline

The Foundation (2014)

Founded on December 17, 2014, by a group of passionate youth, VSAS embarked on a mission to uplift underprivileged communities in Pune.

Skills Development & Career Guidance (2015-16)

Focusing on skill development and career guidance, VSAS empowered youth to build sustainable livelihoods and unlock new opportunities.

Community Empowerment Projects (2017-19)

VSAS expanded its reach, actively involving communities in projects that promote self-sufficiency and long-term well-being.

Sustainability and Long-Term Impact (2020-22)

VSAS’s sustainable programs continued to grow, ensuring long-term empowerment through education, health, and career development.
